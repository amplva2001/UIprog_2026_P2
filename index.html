Crumbs is a human-made collective feed of the web – built entirely from URLs that real people have visited and chosen to leave here. every crumb below is a trace: a site someone went to, tagged, and dropped. When you visit a website, it plants an HTTP cookie in your browser without asking – a file that records your presence, habits, identity. What if you could choose what to leave behind for others to see. Some good news? A good story? A funny fact? The internet is an ever growing space where many hidden treasures will never be discovered and what we see is controlled by algorithms and commercials.... Here, you plant the cookie yourself. you decide what trace you leave and how you name it. paste a URL in the bar below and drop it. Hover any crumb to explore what people have left behind.
